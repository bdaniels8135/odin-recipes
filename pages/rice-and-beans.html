Rice and Beans

Description

Growing up in a diverse town meant having lots of exposure to different foods. Spanish rice and beans was one of the mainstays of my diet even as a white boy. Don't worry though. I am not giving away anyone's family recipe. Just one I developed on my own over the years. I won't tell you how to make the sofrito though. My adoptive abuelo would never forgive me.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups Water
  • 8oz Can Tomato Sauce
  • 2 tsp Chicken Bouillon
  • 3 Tbsp Sofrito
  • 2 Packets of Sazon Seasoning
  • 4 tsp Adobo
  • 4 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 4 Cups White Rice
  • 1 Can Pigeon Peas (Drained and Rinsed)

Steps

  1. Add everything but the rice and peas to a large pot with a thick bottom and lid and bring to a boil.
  2. Add peas and return to boil.
  3. Add rice and stir.
  4. Return contents to a boil and stir the rice a second time.
  5. Even out the rice in the pot.
  6. Put the lid on the pot and reduce the heat to medium low or low.
  7. Let simmer for 20 minutes and turn off the heat. DO NOT TOUCH!
  8. After rice has absorbed the liquid that is resting on the top, fluff with a fork.
  9. Enjoy!
